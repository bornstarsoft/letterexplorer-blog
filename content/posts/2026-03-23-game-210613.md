KONAMI is set to delight fans with the upcoming release of "Picross S: Konami Antiques Collection," a game that promises to blend nostalgia with engaging puzzle-solving. Launching on April 30 for the Nintendo Switch, this new installment will feature 585 puzzles inspired by over 80 classic KONAMI titles, including beloved franchises like Castlevania and Metal Gear. Players will enjoy the challenge of uncovering pixel art sprites from these iconic games, making it a must-have for both puzzle enthusiasts and retro gamers alike.

The game will not only support traditional controls but also offer mouse and touchscreen functionality, catering to a wide range of players. With various puzzle types, including Mega Picross and Color Picross, theres something for everyone to enjoy. As we look forward to its release, one cant help but wonder how this new addition will compare to previous Picross titles and whether it will spark a renewed interest in KONAMI's classic games.
