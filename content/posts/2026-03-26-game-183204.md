Agni-Flare Co., Ltd. has just opened pre-orders for their highly anticipated ultra-high-speed competitive puzzle game, SHIKA-Q, on the Nintendo Switch. Set to launch on April 9, 2026, this game promises to deliver a thrilling experience for puzzle enthusiasts and competitive gamers alike. With its unique mechanics and fast-paced gameplay, SHIKA-Q is poised to become a standout title in the gaming community.

In addition to the Nintendo Switch, SHIKA-Q will also be available on PlayStation4, PlayStation5, and Steam, with the latter version currently under development. This multi-platform release strategy indicates Agni-Flare's commitment to reaching a broad audience and providing gamers with various options to enjoy this exciting new title. As we await its release, the gaming community is buzzing with anticipation for what SHIKA-Q has to offer.
