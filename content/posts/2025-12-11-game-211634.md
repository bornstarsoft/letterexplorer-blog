This December, PlayStation Plus members have a lot to look forward to with the addition of major titles like Assassins Creed Mirage and Wo Long: Fallen Dynasty to the Game Catalog. These games, available to Extra and Premium tier members starting December 16, promise to deliver thrilling gameplay experiences that fans of the franchises have been eagerly anticipating.

In addition to these blockbuster titles, the catalog also features a variety of games that cater to different tastes, including the newly released Skate Story and family-friendly options like Paw Patrol games and Lego Horizon Adventures. Notably, the classic fighter Soulcalibur III joins the Premium Classic Catalog, appealing to nostalgic gamers.
