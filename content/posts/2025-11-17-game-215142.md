Get ready to dive into a chilling world of steampunk horror with the upcoming game, *They Will Come*. Set to release on PC via Steam in 2026, this title from Game Pop Studio promises an immersive experience where players navigate a perilous airship filled with sinister foes known as the Wretched Ones. With a unique blend of stealth, puzzle-solving, and item collection, players will need to rely on their wits and a giant robot companion named Talus to survive.

In *They Will Come*, players assume the role of Benjamin, who must traverse the eerie halls of the airship Endeavor, all while avoiding the relentless Wretched Ones. The games rich narrative unfolds in an alternate history of 1897, where players will uncover a bittersweet story of family and survival amidst a backdrop of stunning steampunk visuals. The attention to detail in the environment, crafted by industry veteran Jeff Troutman, ensures that every corner of the game is a feast for the eyes.

As you prepare for this thrilling adventure, consider how the unique mechanics of stealth and puzzle-solving will challenge your gaming skills. Will you be able to outsmart the Wretched Ones and save your family? The countdown to 2026 has begun, and the world of *They Will Come* is waiting to be explored.
