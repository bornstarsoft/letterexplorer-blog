Jupiter Corporation has exciting news for puzzle game enthusiasts! The beloved title, Juufuutei Radens Guide for Pixel Museum, is set to make its debut on PS4 on December 10th, followed closely by releases for Xbox One and Xbox Series X|S. This game, which originally launched on PC and Nintendo Switch in June 2025, combines the charm of pixel art with engaging puzzles, all guided by the delightful VTuber, Juufuutei Raden.

In this unique gaming experience, players will not only solve puzzles but also immerse themselves in art and culture. With over 300 puzzles of varying sizes, from 5x5 to 40x30, players can enjoy a hands-on exhibition where each solved puzzle contributes to a larger artwork. Curator Raden's voice commentary adds depth, making the journey through the museum both educational and entertaining. The game promises to cater to all skill levels, ensuring that even beginners can join in on the fun.

As we look forward to the release, one can't help but wonder how this blend of gaming and culture will resonate with audiences. Will Juufuutei Radens Guide for Pixel Museum set a new standard for educational gaming? Only time will tell!
