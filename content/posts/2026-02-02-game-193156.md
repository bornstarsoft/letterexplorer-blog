If youre a PlayStation Plus subscriber, theres a hidden gem waiting for you in the form of a chilling puzzle game called The Exit 8. Recently added to the PS Plus Extra and Premium tiers, this indie title has been garnering attention for its unique blend of horror and puzzle-solving mechanics, making it a must-try for fans of the genre.

Developed by Kotake Create, The Exit 8 immerses players in a first-person experience set in a hauntingly repetitive Japanese metro tunnel. Your objective is to identify various anomalies that appear during your loops through the tunnel. These anomalies range from harmless discrepancies to unsettling visuals that can genuinely unnerve players. The challenge lies in spotting these details and reacting quickly to progress, making for an engaging and eerie gameplay experience.

While the game is relatively short, taking just a couple of hours to complete, it offers easy trophies for those looking to achieve 100%. Players have praised its mix of obvious and subtle anomalies, although many wish it could have been longer. As the gaming community continues to explore this indie title, it raises the question: what other hidden gems are waiting to be discovered in the vast library of PlayStation Plus?
