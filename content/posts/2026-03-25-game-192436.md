The gaming world is buzzing with excitement as Starbreeze Entertainment and Fast Travel Games announce the upcoming release of "Payday: Aces High" for multiple VR platforms. With its officially licensed status, fans can expect the same adrenaline-pumping gameplay that the series is known for, but now with the added layer of immersion that VR provides.
