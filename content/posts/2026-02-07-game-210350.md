In a significant move to enhance its global gaming presence, JetSynthesys has acquired EverMerge, a top-grossing merge puzzle game that has generated over $350 million in revenue. This acquisition marks a pivotal step in JetSynthesys' strategy to build a worldwide gaming ecosystem, particularly as it aims to penetrate emerging markets like India. With EverMerge's established success in Western markets, the potential for growth and engagement in new regions is immense.

Rajan Navani, Chairman & MD of JetSynthesys, emphasized the importance of this acquisition in bridging global intellectual properties with local insights. The company plans to leverage its expertise in community engagement and user acquisition to make EverMerge resonate with players in diverse cultural landscapes. This approach not only aims to expand the game's reach but also to enrich the player experience through innovative storytelling and technology.

As JetSynthesys continues to invest in EverMerge, the gaming community can look forward to fresh content and evolving gameplay experiences. This acquisition not only reinforces JetSynthesys' commitment to its players but also positions the company as a formidable player in the global gaming industry.
