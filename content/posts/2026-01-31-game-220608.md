King has just launched Candy Crush Crushable, a daily puzzle game exclusively on Yahoo Games, marking a significant step in the platform's relaunch. This new game offers fresh daily logic challenges, aiming to attract both loyal fans of the Candy Crush franchise and new players looking for engaging content. By integrating daily puzzles, it not only enhances user engagement but also revitalizes Yahoo Games as a competitive platform in the online gaming space.

The introduction of Candy Crush Crushable signifies a broader trend in the gaming industry where established franchises are exploring new formats and platforms to reach audiences. As players increasingly seek daily challenges that fit into their busy lives, this move could set a precedent for how games evolve in the future.
