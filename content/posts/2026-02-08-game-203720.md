If you enjoy puzzle games but prefer a more relaxed experience, then Botany Manor might just be the perfect fit for you. Currently available for free on the Epic Games Store, this charming first-person puzzle game invites players to step into the shoes of botanist Arabella Greene as she explores a Victorian manor in the late 19th century. Unlike other challenging puzzle games, Botany Manor strikes a balance between engaging puzzles and a cozy atmosphere, making it accessible for players who want to enjoy the experience without feeling overwhelmed.

The game's lead developer, Laure De May, emphasizes that the puzzles are designed to be intriguing yet not overly difficult. With a focus on exploration and discovery, players will find themselves immersed in a beautifully crafted world filled with botanical mysteries. The positive reviews on Steam reflect this sentiment, with many players praising its appeal to casual gamers.

This is a unique opportunity to experience Botany Manor for free, as it marks the first time the game has been offered without charge on the Epic Store. So, if you're looking for a delightful puzzle game that won't leave you frustrated, now is the perfect time to dive into Botany Manor. Will you take on the challenge of unraveling its botanical secrets?
