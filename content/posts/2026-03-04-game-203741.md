NHN is making a significant push to revitalize its gaming business by launching two new titles simultaneously, a strategy that could reshape its market presence. The collectible RPG 'Abyssdia' and the puzzle game 'Oshi no Ko: Puzzle Star' are set to capture the attention of gamers worldwide, with both titles achieving remarkable popularity on their launch days. 'Abyssdia' has already claimed the top spot on Google Play in Korea, while 'Puzzle Star' reached #1 on Japan's Apple App Store.

These launches come at a time when market expectations are high due to easing regulations on web board games. However, NHN's focus on publishing and IP licensing raises questions about whether these new releases will lead to substantial profitability. As the gaming landscape evolves, NHN's ability to adapt and innovate will be crucial in determining its future success.

With the gaming industry constantly changing, will NHN's latest titles be enough to turn the tide in their favor? Only time will tell as they navigate this competitive market.
