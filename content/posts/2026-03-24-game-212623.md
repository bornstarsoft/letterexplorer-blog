Cursed Words, the upcoming roguelike puzzle game from Forlife Interactive and Buried Things, is set to release on April 2 on Steam PC. Drawing inspiration from the wildly popular Belatro, this game takes the concept of word creation to a whole new level, blending traditional word games with zany, unpredictable mechanics that promise to keep players engaged.

In Cursed Words, players will use letter tiles to form words, reminiscent of classic games like Scrabble and Boggle. However, the game introduces a twist with its unique rules and abilities, allowing for combinations that include numbers, chess pieces, and other symbols. This innovative approach not only makes the gameplay exciting but also challenges the very essence of what a word game can be.

With 11 playable characters, each boasting distinct powers and synergies, along with over 300 stickers and stamps for customization, Cursed Words offers a plethora of combinations and unique runs. The game is designed to be both fun and unpredictable, making every session a fresh experience. Plus, with a free demo available on Steam, players can dive into the chaos before the official launch.

As we await the release, one can't help but wonder: how will Cursed Words redefine the landscape of word games? Will it attract both traditional gamers and those looking for something new? Only time will tell.
