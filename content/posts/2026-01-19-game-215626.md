SEGA is gearing up for a monumental celebration as Puyo Puyo marks its 35th anniversary in 2026. Following the success of Sonic the Hedgehog's 35th anniversary, the gaming giant is launching the "Dairensa!" project, which pays homage to a beloved song from the Puyo Puyo series. With a plethora of activities planned, fans can expect a vibrant celebration that highlights the game's rich history and its impact on the puzzle genre.

The festivities kick off with a special live stream on February 1, 2026, featuring voice actors, live demos, and updates on the mobile game Puyo Puyo!! Quest. Additionally, fans in Japan can visit pop-up stores in major cities, where they can immerse themselves in the anniversary spirit and snag exclusive merchandise. The collaboration between Sonic and Puyo Puyo promises to add an extra layer of excitement, with a lottery offering unique prizes.

As SEGA continues to innovate and celebrate its iconic franchises, the Puyo Puyo 35th anniversary serves as a reminder of the enduring legacy of puzzle gaming. What other surprises does SEGA have in store for its loyal fans? Stay tuned for more updates!
