The 43rd annual Golden Joystick Awards showcased the best in gaming, with Clair Obscur: Expedition 33 dominating the event by winning all seven awards it was nominated for. This year, over 21 million votes were cast across 22 categories, highlighting the power of fan engagement in determining the winners. The awards not only celebrate outstanding games but also recognize the talent behind them, making it a pivotal moment in the gaming calendar.

Among the notable winners, Ghost of Yotei took home the title of Console Game of the Year, while Hollow Knight: Silksong was celebrated as PC Game of the Year. The event also honored the Hall of Fame inductee, The Sims, reminding us of the enduring legacy of classic games. With such a diverse range of categories, the Golden Joystick Awards continue to reflect the evolving landscape of the gaming industry.

As we look ahead, the excitement builds for next year's awards. Which games will capture our hearts and votes in 2026? The gaming community eagerly awaits the next wave of innovation and storytelling that will shape the future of this beloved medium.
