In an exciting turn of events for Nintendo fans, Walmart may have inadvertently revealed two unannounced GameCube titles set to join the Nintendo Switch Online service. According to a recent discovery, the retailer's website showcased a list of games that included the beloved classics Metroid Prime 2 and Pikmin 2, sparking speculation about their imminent release.

Currently, the Nintendo Switch Online service offers a limited selection of GameCube titles, with only eight games available to Switch 2 users. While Nintendo has confirmed three upcoming titlesSuper Mario Sunshine, Pokmon XD: Gale of Darkness, and Pokmon Colosseumno specific release dates have been provided. The potential addition of Metroid Prime 2 and Pikmin 2 could significantly enhance the library, especially given the nostalgia surrounding these iconic games.
