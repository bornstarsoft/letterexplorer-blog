In a remarkable achievement, Battlefield 6 has been crowned the UK Game of the Year at the UKIE Video Game Awards 2026. This first-person shooter, developed by Battlefield Studios with contributions from Criterion Games, not only snagged the prestigious title but also won the best PC game award, solidifying its place in gaming history. With over seven million copies sold in just three days, it has set a new record for the franchise and emerged as the top-selling game of 2025 in the American market.

The awards ceremony also celebrated other notable titles, including Atomfall, which took home the best UK console game award, and PowerWash Simulator, recognized as the best mobile game. The event highlighted the evolving landscape of gaming, showcasing innovations in mixed reality and user-generated content, with Train Sim World VR: New York and King of Meat respectively receiving accolades in those categories.
