In a world where free games often get overshadowed by big-budget titles, 12, Memory Lane stands out as a remarkable find on Steam. Released on December 15, 2025, this indie game has already garnered "Very Positive" reviews, captivating players with its intriguing narrative and engaging puzzlesall without costing a dime.

Set in the eerie estate of the Villemaine family, players embark on a journey to uncover the mystery behind their disappearance. With a runtime of just under two hours, 12, Memory Lane offers a compact yet emotionally resonant experience that will leave players reflecting long after the credits roll. Developed by students at Rubika Supinfogame under the name Forget-Me-Not Studio, this title proves that even newcomers can deliver compelling storytelling and gameplay.

While some players have noted that the game is better suited for controllers and expressed a desire for a longer experience, the overwhelming positive feedback highlights the potential of indie developers. As we look forward to what Forget-Me-Not Studio will create next, 12, Memory Lane serves as a powerful reminder of the quality and creativity that can emerge from the indie game scene.
