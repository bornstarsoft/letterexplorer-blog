As we approach the end of 2025, Nintendo's Switch 2 is making waves in the gaming community, particularly with its standout titles like Mario Kart World and Donkey Kong Bananza. Both games have been nominated for prestigious awards at the 43rd annual Golden Joystick Awards, showcasing the impressive capabilities of the new console and its exclusive offerings. With nominations for 'Console Game of the Year', 'Best Multiplayer', and 'Best Audio Design', these titles are certainly capturing the attention of gamers worldwide.

The competition is fierce, with other heavyweights like Death Stranding 2 and Ghost of Ytei also in the running. However, the recognition of Mario Kart World and Donkey Kong Bananza highlights not just their quality, but also the potential of the Switch 2 to redefine gaming experiences. As voting is now open, fans have the chance to support their favorite titles and contribute to the excitement surrounding this new gaming era.

With the winners set to be announced on November 20th, the anticipation is palpable. Will Nintendo's latest offerings take home the coveted awards? Only time will tell, but one thing is clear: the Switch 2 is off to a remarkable start in the gaming landscape.
