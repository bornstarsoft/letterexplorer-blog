Remedy Entertainment has officially announced Control Resonant, the highly-anticipated sequel to the award-winning game Control, set to release in 2026. This new action-adventure RPG promises to take players on an exhilarating journey through a warped Manhattan, where they will harness the extraordinary powers of Dylan Faden to battle a cosmic threat that alters reality itself. With an expansive world and a deep progression system, Control Resonant aims to elevate the series to new heights.

In this sequel, players will not only explore the familiar yet transformed landscapes of Manhattan but will also delve into the psychological complexities of Dylan Faden's character. The game introduces a unique gameplay mechanic where players can shape Dylan's abilities, making choices that influence his powers and the environment around him. This level of interactivity and choice is set to redefine the player experience in the Remedy-verse.

As we await the release, the excitement surrounding Control Resonant is palpable. Will this sequel live up to the legacy of its predecessor? Only time will tell, but one thing is for sure: gamers are in for a wild ride in 2026.
